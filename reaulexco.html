Reaulex Co

This website is a portfolio website that I designed for a Creative Director who wanted to display her work. Her porfolio includes all of her services, work, and a contact page that allows people to book appointments. This project was completed in 2 months.

Quote from client = "I love my portfolio! It's clean and sophisticated."

My Role

Branding | Web Design

Platforms Used

Adobe XD | Illustrator | Wix

My Work

Research:

This was my first time designing a portfolio website for a Creative Director, so I did some research for inspiration.

Ideation/Wireframe:

My client had a Pinterest mood board that gave me an idea of the style she wanted for her website. She was going for a minimalist look with a light color palette and a mix of serif + sans serif fonts to create a sophisticated look. We discussed about what she'll need to be on the website such as the content, the imagery, and the type of pages. This is why I chose a cream color as the website's background for the simplicity look and used the brown from the her logo. The light green was used as an accent color to create a call to action for the buttons and make certain sections' styles standout.
I came up with a simple information architecture that shows the website's navigation.


Prototype:

I used Adobe XD to design the website and Wix.com to code it. I also used Illustrator to create some plant illustraions as a background graphic in some of the pages. The final website looks slightly different from the original prototype but the website successfully markets her Creative Directing skills with a specialization in photography.
