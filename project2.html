Reaulex Co

This portfolio website was designed for a Creative Director to showcase her work, services, and include a contact page for booking appointments. Completed in two months, the project was my first time designing for a Creative Director, so I researched for inspiration, guided by her Pinterest mood board. She wanted a minimalist style with a light color palette, combining serif and sans-serif fonts for sophistication. We collaborated on the website's content, imagery, and structure, leading to a simple information architecture. The site features a cream background for simplicity, brown accents from her logo, and light green highlights for calls to action.
