TY Guys Emergency Roadside

A service website for a new business owner that provides roadside emergency services. The client wanted a website that will display his work, showcase the services that he provides, and include ways for clients to reach out to him.

My Role

Branding | Web Design | Graphic Design

Platforms Used

Figma | Illustrator | Wix

My Work

Research:

When tackling this project, I looked at other car services and competitors' websites to better understand the structure and trends that these businesses follow to create a successful website. From there I was able to make decisions that will make the website more effective such as having their phone number on a sticky banner at the top so it's easily acessible. Also including the services that are provided and dedicating section that explains why my clients services are reliable.

Ideation/Wireframe:

I noticed that other car service websites were relatively simple but straight to the point, so I tried emulating that in my design. Since it was a new business, I decided to go with a one-pager to allow clients to easily access information quickly.


Prototype:

I used Adobe XD to design the website and Wix.com to code it. I also used Illustrator to customize some of the services icons.
