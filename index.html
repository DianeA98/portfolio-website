About Diane

My Story

Based in Georgia, I'm a Jr. Web Designer at Ballard Designs as well as a Graphic Designer and an aspiring Front-End Developer. I'm passionate about art, design, and fashion. I enjoy traveling, listening to music, and I speak three languages. I specialize in website design, HTML/CSS, UX/UI Design and graphic design.

I started this journey when I was about to graduate from high school and couldn't figure out what major to choose. The tech world was interesting to me and I already loved art, so finding a major that combined the two was perfect. Fast forward to 2023, I've dabbled in a few things. From graphic design, some animation, web design, UX/UI, and even photography.
