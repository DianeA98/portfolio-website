HER Website

A responsive and fan-based website, this was a class project and the prompt was to create a multi-page website of any subject of our choice. I chose an artist who already has a website, so I challenged myself to redesign the website to how I believe would have worked better. This website is for anyone that would like to know more about the artist, listen to her latest music and view her pictures.
